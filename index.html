Effortless Living: Connectivity & Cultured Leisure.

Barnes Green offers the coveted "Wirral commuter's dream"—a peaceful family haven with direct access to dynamic city careers and an abundance of high-quality leisure options.

Seamless Commutes

For professionals working in Liverpool or Chester, Spital Station is a revelation. A short walk from your door, it offers fast, reliable Merseyrail services, transforming your daily commute into a stress-free journey.

  • Liverpool Lime Street: ~22 minutes
  • Chester City Centre: ~25 minutes
  • M53 Motorway: 5 minutes
  • Clatterbridge Hospital: 5-10 minutes

Cultured Leisure

Indulge in the vibrant cultural scenes of Liverpool and Chester. Enjoy world-class dining, theatres, and museums just a short train ride away, perfect for sophisticated evenings out or enriching family days.

  • Fine Dining: Liverpool's Albert Dock, Chester's city centre.
  • Arts & Culture: Tate Liverpool, World Museum, Storyhouse Chester.
  • Local Gems: Lady Lever Art Gallery, Port Sunlight Village.

Tranquil Outdoors

The Wirral Peninsula is renowned for its natural beauty. Enjoy family days out at the award-winning Chester Zoo, explore scenic beaches, or unwind with a round of golf at one of the area's prestigious courses.

  • Family Fun: Chester Zoo (20 min drive).
  • Coastal Walks: Thurstaston, West Kirby, Parkgate.
  • Green Spaces: Wirral Country Park, Royden Park.
  • Golf: Multiple local clubs, including Royal Liverpool.
